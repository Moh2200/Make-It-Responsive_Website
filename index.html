Company description:

Enova is a company that develops, produces, and sells laptops in developing countries.
We have our HQ in Oslo, but the production is in New Dehli India.

specialised in this products

laptops, mobile phones, and solar system which are among essenials in developing countries.

laptops

Reduce CPU from Intel to AMD or ARM. The screens are manufactured LCD 720p to 1080p. 128 Gb of SSD storage!

mobile phones

Reduced the GPU and CPU to ARM and to lower specifies snapdragons, LCD panels, smaller 2800 MhA battery, normal 2A 5V battery charging, cameras are reduced to one 12 Megapixel sensor.

solar system for houses

the DC to AC converters are located on the solar panel and are long livable and easy to replace. The solar panels are manufactured with a new revolutionary technology that reduced the cost by 90%.
